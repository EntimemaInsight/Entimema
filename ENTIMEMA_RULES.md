# ENTIMEMA OS — Core Rules

These rules are the source of truth for every Entimema digital and visual asset.

1. The first desktop viewport must present a complete, intentional composition.
2. The logo mark must be vector-based; the wordmark must remain live text.
3. No visual element may be scaled as a screenshot or rasterized page.
4. Brand colors must come from `styles/tokens.css`.
5. Typography must use the defined hierarchy and no arbitrary font sizes.
6. Spacing follows the 8 px rhythm unless an optical correction is documented.
7. Header height must remain compact and must not dominate the hero.
8. The hero must state one clear promise and no more than five supporting pillars.
9. Dots are compositional accents, not a repeating wallpaper.
10. Every page must preserve generous whitespace and clear hierarchy.
11. Components are reused before new one-off markup is introduced.
12. Desktop, tablet and mobile are designed deliberately; mobile is not a scaled desktop.
13. Motion must be subtle, purposeful and respect reduced-motion preferences.
14. Accessibility and readability take priority over decorative effects.
15. Any exception must be documented in the changelog.
